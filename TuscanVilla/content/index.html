LOCATIONS

Some places for you to go

Location #1

Farmhouse in Lucca Area, Tuscany, Italy. This renovated Tuscan farmhouse sits on a 2km gravel drive above the ancient hillside town of Barga. It is secluded yet within easy reach (20 mins) of both Barga and Castlenuovo with their many facilities for eating and entertainment. The villa has 3 double bedrooms (1 x en-suite), 2 further bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, living room, terrace and pool area.

Location #2

Villa Naxos – 3,700 sq ft of interior space, is where all of this becomes a reality. This welcoming ocean front property is set in a quiet part of the lively Naxos, Taormina’s beach town. It boasts contemporary interiors, private access to the black volcanic rocky beach below and a superb location. It is only a few minute drive from Naxos with its shops, restaurants and a larger equipped beach. The villa features its own private wellness space.

Location #3

Three private luxury residences set on a 900 acre estate with breath-taking views overlooking vineyards and olive groves near Florence. Large immaculate living areas, air-conditioned bedrooms with en suite bathrooms compliment the original features. Two private fenced pools, a football pitch and astro-turf tennis court all within a unique unspoilt setting. English spoken.
